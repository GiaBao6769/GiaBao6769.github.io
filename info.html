The meaning behind my avatar

I made this avatar about 2 years ago (2023). Back then, I'm really into transcripting music, so much so that I've spent hours just sitting in front of the screen adjusting notations in Musescore. That was the inspiration for this image, it's a converted treble clef mingling with a bass clef in order to form a shape like letter "B." In the first version, there was no sharp, but then I came up with that idea when I learned about Key signatures to transcript Field of Memories by Waterflame (you can check out that in my Musescore account). A sharp key will turn the whole section to G major, and combine with the B shape, we get GB, which stands for Gia Bao.
